Which Type of Computer?

Select which type of computer you would like to begin building, based on the needs of your new job:

  • Frequent travel
  • Maintaining active and personal contact with customers and sales representatives in your district, including setting up conference calls using software such as Microsoft Outlook
  • Submitting monthly reports on sales in your district using software such as Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Word
  • Maintaining an online presence with clients, making them comfortable with electronic communication as well as face-to-face contact
  • Creating, editing, and sharing multimedia presentations, customized for each client, which can be viewed on the web or on your own computer using software such as Microsoft PowerPoint and/or Adobe Photoshop
