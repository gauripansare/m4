Processor:

  • 1 GHz or faster x86-bit or x64-bit processor

RAM:

  • 1 GB RAM (32-bit)
  • 2 GB RAM (64-bit)

Hard Drive:

  • 3 GB of available hard-disk space for installation

Video Card:

  • 1024 x 576 or higher resolution monitor
